Caesar Chicken Wrap

Savor the goodness of our Healthy Chicken Caesar Wrap – grilled chicken, crisp romaine lettuce, and a light Caesar dressing, all nestled in a whole-grain wrap. This guilt-free delight combines taste and nutrition, offering a quick and wholesome option for a satisfying meal that supports your well-being. Enjoy without compromise!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 375°. On a rimmed baking sheet, toss bread with 1 teaspoon olive oil, ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, ¼ teaspoon salt, and ¼ teaspoon pepper. Spread in a single layer and bake until golden and crisp, 10 to 12 minutes.

step 2

Meanwhile, season chicken on all sides with oregano and remaining ½ teaspoon garlic powder, ½ teaspoon salt, and ½ teaspoon pepper. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat remaining 1 tablespoons oil until shimmering. Cook chicken, turning occasionally, until both sides have a nice sear and an instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of breast registers 165º, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

step 3

Transfer chicken to a cutting board. Once cool enough to handle, slice chicken into strips.

step 4

In a large bowl, toss chicken, croutons, lettuce, Parmesan, and desired amount of Caesar dressing; season with salt and pepper

step 5

Divide salad among tortillas and wrap up burrito-style. Slice in half and serve.

ingredients

1 cup white bread, cut into 1/2" cubes

1 tbsp plus 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

3/4 tsp garlic powder, divided

3/4 tsp Kosher salt, divided

3.4 tsp freshly groud black pepper, divided

1 large chicken breast, sliced in half lengthwise

1/2 tsp dried oregano

1 head romain lettuce, chopped

2 tbsp to 1/4 cup store-bought of homemade Caesar dressing

1/4 cup of finely grated parmesean

4 whole grain tortillas
